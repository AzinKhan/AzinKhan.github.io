All My Crimes

I lose myself
And taste the sweetest sin

Intoxication. Whether it is lust, power, devotion, or good old fashioned liquor, there is something that holds this power over all of us. We crave it, we fear it, and we can hate ourselves because of it. This theme of intoxication runs through many strands of music ranging from from Rock ’n’ Roll, Hip-Hop, even religious Qawwali music. 

All My Crimes is an attempt to add to this canon with everything turned up to 11. Long a mainstay of our live shows, the recorded version has been overdue for some time. The challenge was to capture the essence of this song with its almighty drums, buzzing guitars, and shredded lead vocals.
We like to keep it simple so there isn’t anything else going on except those drums, drop-D guitars, and a handful of vocal overdubs but this song’s emotional and dynamic range proved difficult to capture effectively. After a lot of mixes, it’s our pleasure to bring you this exclusive release at last. Turn it up!
