Automatic Panic are Azin Khan and Andy Payne.

They have made it their mission to sound bigger than any two-piece has the right to.

Inspired by raucous duos like The White Stripes and Royal Blood, alongside bands including Clutch and Queens of the Stone Age, they have spent their first few years writing and performing in London and the surrounding area. Their debut EP, Automatic Panic, was followed by a series of singles, gathering attention slowly and surely with each release.

2021 was a strong year for the Panic, with the launch of a mailing-list-only EP, a live double A side, a podcast called the Music Book Club, and a triumphant return to the stage booked for the summer.

2022 promises more, with a full album, Altered States, currently in production.

In live settings, the twosome blend an intense stage presence with exceptionally tight performance - notable quotes include Azin being dubbed "the best frontman in London" and a new fan reflecting that "it took me four songs to realise there were only two of you".
